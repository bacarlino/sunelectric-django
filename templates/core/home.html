We have years of experience with electrical installations in a wide variety of industrial environments. From manufacturing facilities with intricate machinery and conveyor belt systems to complex water treatment facilities - we have the knowledge and background to provide an accurate, professional, and quality installation of all aspects of the electrical system. Sun Electric has been providing high-quality commercial electrical work around central Indiana since it's inception. From multi-million dollar new-construction buildings to a simple receptacle addition in your office break-room, from start to finish we get the job done skillfully and accurately.

SERVICE
